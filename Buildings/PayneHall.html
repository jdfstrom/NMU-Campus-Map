Payne Hall

Residents of Payne Hall continue their learning outside of the classroom. Payne Hall provides residents with an academically focused atmosphere in all of its houses. Also included in Payne Hall is an academic house where residents are expected to maintain at least a 2.75 GPA throughout the academic year. Payne Hall provides a safe, friendly environment that promotes education, fellowship, and community. Volunteering in the Marquette community is a focus for Payne Hall residents and is apparent in their dedication to the Superior Edge Program at NMU. Payne Hall is made up of one all male house, one all female house, and four co-ed housess.
