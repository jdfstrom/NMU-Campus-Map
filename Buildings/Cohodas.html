Cohodas

The tallest building on Northern’s campus, Cohodas Hall, houses many academic departments and administrative offices, as well as community outreach centers. These include:

  • Academic
    • College of Business
    • Economics, History, Philosophy and Political Science Departments
    • Graduate Studies
    • Academic Affairs
  • Administrative
    • Office of the President
    • Finance and Administration
  • Outreach
    • Alumni Association
    • Communications and Marketing
    • Continuing Education
    • NMU Foundation
    • Northern Center for Lifelong Learning

Several service departments formerly housed in Cohodas Hall are now located in C.B. Hedgcock, including admissions, financial aid, registration and scheduling, and the registrar’s office.

The building is named after Upper Peninsula banker and philanthropist Sam M. Cohodas.
