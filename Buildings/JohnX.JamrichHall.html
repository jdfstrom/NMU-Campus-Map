John X. Jamrich Hall

Newly built Jamrich Hall is a classroom and academic department building, containing a 500-seat lecture hall with a stage, three 80-seat lecture rooms with tiered seating and 24 active learning classrooms designed to encourage collaborative learning.There are also five academic department offices: English, criminal justice, sociology and anthropology, social work and math and computer science. Additional features include a Starbucks restaurant, six informal learning areas, and a connector walkway to the Learning Resources Center.

The building is named after former NMU President John X. Jamrich (1968-1983).
